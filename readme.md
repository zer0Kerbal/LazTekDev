# LazTek Dev (LTD)

Agent, flags and common configs for LazTek.



## By [`zer0Kerbal`][zer0Kerbal], inspired by [`LazarusLuan`][lazarusluan]

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/zer0Kerbal/LazTekDev/master/img/HeroLogo_1920x1920.png" alt="LazTekDev Hero" width="50%" height="50%">

### Preamble by [LazarusLuan][[lazarusluan]

> LazTek Temporal Anomalies is pleased to announce the availability of our LazTek Launch Pack, LazTek Exploration Expansion kit, LazTek Colonial Transporter and LazTek Historical Archive for Kerbal Space Program.
> Available for download in low, medium and high resolution versions, these mods include replicas of the new LazTek Dragon V2 capsule and trunk, LazTek Falcon 9 v1.1 and Falcon Heavy launch platforms, proposed Colonial Transporter, plus special configurations for exploration, asteroid redirection, refueling, colonization and more! Add the LazTek Historic Archive for access to the original DragonRider concept spacecraft.
